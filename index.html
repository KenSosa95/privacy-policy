We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services, including the following:

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us or via third-party authentication services, such as Google, Facebook, or Apple Sign-In. This information includes:

  • Email address
  • First and last name
  • Postal address (including City, State, Province, and ZIP/Postal Code)

Device Information

  • Location data (precise GPS location)
  • Camera data (photos and videos you capture and upload through the app)

How To Delete Your Data

  • To delete your data and account follow these steps:
  • Go to Profile > Configuration > Delete Account > Confirm > Introduce Your User ID
  • All your data is deleted

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect for various purposes related to providing and improving our services, including but not limited to:

  • Authentication and Account Management: We use OAuth services such as Google Sign-In, Facebook Connect, and Apple Sign-In to facilitate seamless and secure authentication. The information provided by these services (such as your name, email, and profile picture) is used to create and manage your account in our app.
  • Service Enhancement: Your information helps us personalize your experience, improve app functionality, and understand how users interact with the app.
  • Communication: We may use your email address and other contact details to communicate with you about important updates, account verification, and promotional content (with your consent). You can opt out of promotional communications at any time by following the unsubscribe link provided in these communications.
  • Payments and Purchases: If you make purchases within the app, we will process payments through third-party services like Apple Store In-App Payments and Google Play In-App Payments. We do not store your payment information.
  • Advertising: We may show personalized advertisements based on your interactions within the app. Third-party advertising services may use your information to display relevant ads.
  • Analytics and Performance Tracking: We use tools like Google Analytics to track app usage and monitor user behavior. This information helps us identify performance issues, optimize app features, and improve overall user experience.

6. Your Privacy Rights

California Residents (CCPA and CPRA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), California residents have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request access to the personal data we have collected.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of personal data, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of personal data (we do not sell your data).

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@eapp-services.com.

European Economic Area (GDPR)

If you are a resident of the EEA, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right to Access and Rectification: Request access to or rectification of your personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request the deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your personal data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for the processing of your data at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@eapp-services.com.
